FAQ

Short answers to the stuff Apple reviewers and users will ask.

Does JacketCast need my location?

Location is optional, but recommended. If you allow it, JacketCast uses your location to fetch the forecast for your current area. You can revoke location access anytime in iOS Settings.

How does the recommendation work?

JacketCast compares near-term forecast conditions against your thresholds. Adjust temperature and sensitivity settings to match how you personally experience “jacket weather.”

Is JacketCast Plus a subscription?

No. It’s a $2.99 one-time purchase.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Support → Restore Purchase. Apple may require you to be signed into the same Apple ID used to purchase.

What data do you collect?

JacketCast does not require accounts. App preferences live on-device. Ads may involve third-party providers (free tier). See the Privacy Policy for details.
