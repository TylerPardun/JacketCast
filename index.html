FAQ

  • How does JacketCast decide?
    It compares forecast conditions against your settings (temperature, and optionally wind/rain). The decision is based on a short rolling time window so it can account for changes soon after you leave.
  • Why do I need Location?
    Location is used to fetch weather for your current area. You can disable Location and use a saved location if available in your version.
  • Can I turn off ads?
    Yes — the paid tier removes ads.

Troubleshooting

  • Weather won’t load
    Check cellular/Wi-Fi, then try force quitting and reopening the app.
  • Notifications not arriving
    Go to Settings → Notifications → JacketCast and enable notifications. Also confirm reminders are enabled inside the app.
  • Restore purchase
    In the app’s Settings, tap Restore Purchase. If it still doesn’t restore, email support with your device type and iOS version.
