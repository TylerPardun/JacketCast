Help, troubleshooting, and purchase restore

If anything feels off, start here. If you still need help, email contact.jacketcast@gmail.com.

Common fixes

Weather won’t load

Confirm internet access. Then force-quit and reopen the app. If you’re on VPN, try disabling it.

Location issues

iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → JacketCast → “While Using the App.”

Notifications not arriving

iOS Settings → Notifications → JacketCast → allow notifications. Also confirm reminders are enabled inside the app.

Restore purchase

If you previously bought JacketCast Plus and it’s not showing as active:

  1. Open JacketCast → Settings
  2. Tap Restore Purchase
  3. Confirm you’re signed into the same Apple ID used to purchase

If it still doesn’t restore, email contact.jacketcast@gmail.com with your device model, iOS version, and what you’re seeing.

How the decision works

JacketCast compares forecast conditions against your thresholds. If conditions are below your comfort settings (and optional factors like wind/rain are enabled), it recommends bringing a jacket.

Your thresholds are personal. If the app feels “too conservative,” raise your temperature threshold or adjust wind/rain sensitivity.
