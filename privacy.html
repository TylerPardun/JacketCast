Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Summary

  • No account required.
  • Location is used only if you grant permission, to fetch weather.
  • Free tier may show ads via third-party providers.
  • Purchases are handled by Apple (StoreKit).

Information JacketCast uses

  • Location (optional): Used to fetch weather for your current area. You can revoke permission anytime in iOS Settings.
  • App settings: Thresholds and preferences are stored locally on your device (and in the shared widget container if enabled).
  • Purchase status: Stored to enable features such as ad removal after buying JacketCast Plus.

Third-party services

  • Advertising providers (free tier) may collect device identifiers and interaction data to measure ad performance and prevent fraud, subject to iOS settings and their policies.
  • Apple StoreKit handles in-app purchases.
  • Weather APIs supply forecast data for recommendations.

Third-party providers control their own data practices. Your use of those services is governed by their policies.

Tracking and personalized ads

JacketCast may show non-personalized ads by default. If personalized ads are enabled, iOS may show an App Tracking Transparency prompt. You can control tracking and ad personalization in iOS Settings.

Data sharing

JacketCast does not sell your personal information. The app may share limited information with third parties only to provide ads, purchases, and app functionality as described above.

Your choices

  • Location: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services
  • Notifications: iOS Settings → Notifications → JacketCast
  • Tracking: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact contact.jacketcast@gmail.com.
